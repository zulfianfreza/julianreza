dudukberbagi is a dedicated fundraising platform for Indonesia. We are here to connect compassionate individuals who want to provide financial assistance and support to various social, charitable, and meaningful initiatives in Indonesia. Through DudukBerbagi, you can discover a wide range of fundraising campaigns that support various causes, from education and healthcare to humanitarian aid and environmental preservation. We facilitate the fundraising process with ease, security, and transparency, ensuring that every contribution you make has a tangible impact on society. Join our community at DudukBerbagi, and together, we sit, share, and bring meaning to Indonesia.

### Tech Stack

- Next.js 13
- Tailwind CSS
- Typescript
- Prisma
- PostgreSQL
- NextAuth
- Zustand
- Cloudinary
